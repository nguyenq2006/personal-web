SJSU Book Finder

April, 2016

Check-in System

November, 2016

As a Supplemental Instruction leader, I have to keep track of all the students that come to my session, so the professor can give them their participation points at the end of the semester. I used Google spreadsheet to keep track of all the students, but because of confidentiality, I can’t let the students see came to my session before and how many points they have. Every session, I had to create and new spreadsheet, and at the end of the semester I had to go through all the spreadsheet and calculate the point. This is when I decided to make application that help me keep track of the students, and make it easier for them to check in. The check-in system all the students to check-in using their ID. In addition, the system keep track of all the points, and which section the student attended.
